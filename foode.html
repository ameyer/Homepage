Foode

Foode is a service that designs a week's worth of meals to fit your eating habits. Foode does this by getting to know every detail about what the user likes to eat, how long they have to cook, how experienced they are and how much money they have to spend per meal. Once designed, Foode then delivers all the groceries needed to make the meals. Included in the delivery are customized recipe cards and a food report so the user can see not only how much each meal cost to make, but how much it cost per person.

After the user has made the meals, they can rate them on the Foode website. This gives Foode a better understanding about what they liked and didn't like.

Work by Adam Meyer and Molly Rosenberg for Matt Cottam, and Maia Garau's Service Design Class at RISD

Twitter
Counter

As a promotional piece for bildr I created this simple box to display the current count of bildr's Twitter followers.

The device connects to Twitter's APIs every half minute to get and update of the number of followers and displays it on the Soviet-era nixie tubes. The case is made out of layered pieces of laser-cut plywood, and then painted and sanded some dozen times to remove any indication of the layers.

I'm a designer with a love of software.

I went to RISD for Industrial Design. While I was there, this funny thing started happening. All my favorite products became software, or a case for it. I wasn't prepared for this.

I see code, like wood, as just another material. But the truly exciting thing is that long before I was around, the limits of wood, metal, and plastics had been pushed, tested and broken. But every day code is pushed further, and new concepts are created. The limitation of the material is like none we have ever seen.

So here I am, a designer like many designers before me, ready to push the boundaries of material.
